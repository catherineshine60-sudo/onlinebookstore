Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns for all books in good condition. Simply contact our support team to initiate the return process.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. We also offer express shipping for orders that need to arrive faster.

Do you offer international shipping?

no! currently,we only provide shipping within Tirunelveli and surrounding areas

Can I change my order?

If your order hasn't been processed, contact us immediately. We'll do our best to help you make changes.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently we operate online only, but you can visit our showroom by appointment. Call us for details.

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Use it to monitor your delivery status.
