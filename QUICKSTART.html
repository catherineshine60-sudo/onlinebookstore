🚀 Professional Online Book Store - Quick Start Guide

⚡ Getting Started in 30 Seconds

Step 1: Open the index.html file in your web browser
Step 2: Browse books, use search/filters, add to cart
Step 3: Enjoy! Your cart saves automatically

📖 Available Pages

  • index.html - Home page with featured books and hero section
  • books.html - Complete catalog with search & filters
  • book-details.html - Detailed information about a specific book
  • categories.html - Browse books by category
  • cart.html - Shopping cart with checkout
  • wishlist.html - Saved favorite books
  • profile.html - Customer profile & settings
  • about.html - Company information & team
  • contact.html - Contact form & location
  • chatbot.html - AI assistant for recommendations

✨ Key Features

🌙 Dark Mode

Click the moon icon to toggle between dark and light themes. Your preference is saved!

🛒 Shopping Cart

Add books to cart, adjust quantities, and see totals. Data persists between sessions.

❤️ Wishlist

Save favorite books for later. Your wishlist is saved automatically.

🔍 Smart Search

Search by title, author, or category. Real-time filtering available.

📱 Responsive

Perfect on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Works offline after first load.

🤖 Chatbot

AI-powered assistant for book recommendations and customer support.

🎯 How to Use Each Feature

Shopping

1. Go to Books page or Home page
2. Click "Add to Cart" on any book
3. Go to Cart page to review
4. Click "Checkout" to complete order

Wishlist

1. Click the heart icon (❤️) on any book
2. Go to Wishlist page to view favorites
3. Move items to cart anytime

Search & Filter

1. Use search bar in navigation to search instantly
2. On Books page, use filters to narrow results
3. Sort by price, rating, or title

Dark Mode

1. Click the moon icon (🌙) in top navigation
2. Theme changes instantly
3. Your preference is saved automatically

Chatbot

1. Go to Chatbot page
2. Type your question or request
3. Get instant recommendations and answers
