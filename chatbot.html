Common Questions

❓ Q: How do I search for books?

Use the search bar in the navigation menu or visit our Books page to browse and filter by category, price, or rating.

❓ Q: Can I save books for later?

Yes! Click the heart icon on any book card to add it to your wishlist. You can view your wishlist anytime.

❓ Q: How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Use it to monitor your delivery status.

❓ Q: Is there a dark mode?

Yes! Click the moon icon in the top navigation to switch to dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

❓ Q: Do you offer gift cards?

Currently gift cards are available through our customer support team. Contact us for more information.

❓ Q: Can I subscribe to newsletters?

Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter in the footer section to get the latest book recommendations and exclusive offers.
